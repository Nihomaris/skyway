Almaty – Wonderful city of Apples

In only two days you will get chance to explore Almaty, feel the spirit of the apple city & admire the majestic mountain views.


Ski resort «Shymbulak» is located in the picturesque ravine of Zailyiskyi Alatau on the height of 2260 meters above the sea level and only 25 kilometers from the center of Almaty. Entertaining activities, cozy cafes and outstanding atmosphere is waiting for you here.


Kok-tobe is so-called the city mountain of Almaty. There is a popular recreation area on top of the mountain. Kok-Tobe is one of the main landmarks of the city. The viewpoint from Kok-Tobe is well worth a visit with panoramic views in all directions.


The First President park will amaze you with its singing fountains, a real Japanese garden with pagodas and lanterns and the observation deck, which offers a breathtaking view of the city, Ile Alatau and the Big Almaty peaks.


We offer you to visit the unique "Sunkar" nursery of rare endangered species of birds. By visiting falcon nursery you can admire the most beautiful birds, learn about their biological characteristics, and more than that, see the falcon show. The trained birds take parts in that show and demonstrate hunting.


After that you will visit Big Almaty Lake. It is located at an altitude of 2500 meters above sea level, among the steep peaks of the Big Almaty gorge. Like most of the Tien-Shan mountain lakes, it was formed as a result of a strong earthquake. The unique nature abounds with its flora and fauna, and fascinating landscapes remain in the memory of tourists for a long time.
